Useful Websites for UI Design: A Complete Guide

Are you a UI/UX designer looking to expand your toolkit? This article lists some websites that have been useful for me when I started out in website design.

1. Font Pairings and Fonts for Design

Choosing the right typography can elevate your design. These sites provide free or affordable font options and tools:

  • Font Pair: Find perfect font pairings for any project.
  • DaFont: Browse and download unique fonts to integrate into your designs.
  • Google Fonts: A library of open-source fonts ready to use for web and mobile designs.
  • Free Korean Font: Korean fonts for multilingual projects.
  • Transfonter: A free tool to convert TTF fonts into WOFF for web use.

2. Color Palette Tools

Explore these tools for generating and testing palettes:

  • Coolors: Quickly create and explore stunning color palettes.
  • Palette Maker: A simple tool to build, adjust, and save your color schemes.

3. Website and App Design Inspiration

Stuck in a creative rut? These platforms offer inspiration from top-tier designs:

  • Squarespace: Browse templates to spark your creativity.
  • Awwwards: Discover award-winning website designs from around the globe.
  • Pinterest: A treasure trove of UI/UX ideas for design layouts and aesthetics.
  • Mobbin: A curated library of iOS app designs for mobile inspiration.
  • Built for Mars: Bite-sized case studies highlighting excellent UX design.

4. Essential Tools for Developers

Make your development process seamless with this must-have tool:

  • Devv.ai: A developer-focused search engine to find solutions and insights for your coding challenges.

5. Graphic Design Tools

Add finishing touches to your designs with these handy resources:

  • PhotoScissors: Easily remove backgrounds to make images transparent.
  • HTML Symbols: Access heart symbols, emojis, and HTML codes for your web projects.

6. Animations and Interactivity

Animations can bring your design to life. Use this resource to add a dynamic touch:

  • LottieFiles: A library of high-quality, lightweight animations that integrate seamlessly into your designs.
