Korean Words I Find Funny/Interesting

불알친구

Literal translation: “testicle friends”
Actual meaning: “friends since diapers”, "crib mates"


물고기

Literal translation: “water meat”
Actual meaning: “fish”


헌팅갈래

Literal translation: “do you want to go hunting”
Actual meaning: “do you wanna go pick up girls”


탠션 업!

Literal translation: “tension up!”
Actual meaning: “let’s get excited/hype!”


바람 피우다

Literal translation: “to let the wind spread”, “to smoke the wind”
Actual meaning: “to cheat on someone”, “have an affair”


킹 받아

Literal translation: "to accept the king"
Actual meaning: "to get frustrated" (similar to 열받다 )
